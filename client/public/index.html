WiFicLAN

On a planetary scale, information generated by Internet and phone usage “IRL” accrues in the cloud and on the servers of government agencies. Binary bits with hyperlinks to coordinates and unique IDs, grains that map invisibly onto reality. Digital sediment settles, grows, and becomes dimensional. But we lack the means to adequately view and make use of this dimension.
